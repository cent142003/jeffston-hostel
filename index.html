About Jeffston Court Hostel

At Jeffston Court Hostel, we’re redefining student living by offering a home away from home. Located in the peaceful Adenta Court Complex, our facility is tailored for students seeking comfort, safety, and academic focus in Accra.

Our rooms are fully furnished, air-conditioned, and equipped with Smart TVs and study essentials. We offer high-speed Wi-Fi, uninterrupted power and water, and shared kitchens to make life convenient for every resident.

With added benefits like on-site transport, 24/7 staff support, and wellness partnerships for gym, medical, and food services, Jeffston Court Hostel ensures you're supported throughout your stay.
